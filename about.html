MESSAGE FROM THE CHAIRMAN

Why can others do it but we can’t?

Why do others succeed that we fail?

Why can other countries do it but we can’t?

I have been relentlessly searching days and nights with the firm belief that there will be the answers for those questions. After all, the answers turn out to be the core, the goal of the Vision, and the Responsibility that Trung Nguyen has been pursuing for the past twenty-two years. That is, let’s serve our Brothers, Sisters, organization, community, nation, peoples, and the entire human race with Good and Righteous Heart. Each of us must engrave on mind that, when serving with Good and Righteous heart in every single work and moment, then no matter how big the expectation of the organization, community, nation, peoples, mankind is, or no matter how big or small dreams of each individual are, they all will finally come true.

We affirm that the road to Success and True Happiness is no other than Serving with Love. This is the only highest and the most important energy for everyone and every organization that I consider as the Hearted Energy of Serving. When we truly serve our Brothers, Sisters, including the most vulnerable ones to friends, customers, partners, communities, nations, and peoples, we will get thousand times more in return.

We always believe that, “If the others can, we can; if the other countries can, Vietnam can, and can even do better. We definitely can!”

Maybe we the Brothers and Sisters all want the organization, community, and the country, where we are serving and living, to become a role-model sample; and we ourselves certainly wish to become preeminent to accomplish our lifetime great ambitions. From the early days of start-up, Trung Nguyen’s journey has always been the journey of Serving the community, and this commitment is being strongly promoted through the “The Journey of Setting Great Mindset – Start-up – Building the Nation for Vietnamese Youth” in order to bring to the community not only the perfect cups of Energy Coffee, but also the Formula of Success for each individual, family, organization, community, and country by the valuable Life-changing Books. So, do not wait anymore! Let’s join the Serving Journey with our very Good and Righteous Heart right through our usual daily work, which are also extremely meaningful from now on.

IF YOU WANT TO SUCCEED, THERE MUST BE ASPIRATIONS!
IF YOU WANT TO BE HAPPY, YOU MUST SERVE WITH HEART!
THE HIGHER RESPONSIBILITY, THE BIGGER GLORY!
TOGETHER, NOTHING IMPOSSIBLE!
